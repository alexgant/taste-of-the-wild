Chilly Elixir

Icy Blue

"Grants a high-level cooling effect, raising your body's reistance to heat. Crucial for long journeys through the desert."

Ingredients

  • 1 part Blue Curacao
  • 1 part Vodka
  • 4 parts lemonade

Directions

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Pour vodka and blue curacao in a shaker with ice.
  3. Shake well and strain into the highball glass.
  4. Top with lemonade and garnish with a orange or lemon slice if desired.

Fairy Tonic

Pink

"This powerful recovery elixir harnesses the power of fairies. it has a sweet fragrance."

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces white whiskey
  • 3/4 ounce Grand Marnier
  • 1 Tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp blackberry preserves
  • 3 fresh blackberries, more for garnish
  • lime peel twist

Directions

  1. Fill an old-fashioned glass with crushed ice.
  2. Pour first 5 ingredients into an ice-filled cocktail shaker.
  3. Shake well, about 40-50 seconds.
  4. Strain into the old-fashioned glass.
  5. Garnish with extra berries and lime twist.

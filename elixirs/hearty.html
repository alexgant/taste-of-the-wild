Hearty Elixir

Red

"Restores you to full health and increases your maximum hearts."

Ingredients

Serves 4
  • 4 cups ice
  • 12 oz Coca Cola or other cola
  • 3/4 cup bourbon
  • 3/4 cup grenadine

Directions

  1. Put 4 - 10 oz glasses in to the freezer to chill for 15 minutes or more.
  2. Put all ingredients into blender. Blend about 1 minute until slushy and smooth.
  3. Pour into chilled glass and serve immediately. Garnish with maraschino cherry (preferably Luxardo) if desired.

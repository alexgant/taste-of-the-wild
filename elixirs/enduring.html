Enduring Elixir

Bright Green

"Restores stamina and temporarily extends your stamina wheel."

Ingredients

Serves 3
  • 6 ounces gin
  • 1 cup cucmber, cut into chunks, plus a few thin slices for garnish
  • 12 mint leaves, plus more for garnish
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Club soda, chilled
  • ice

Directions

  1. In a blender combine cucumber chunks, mint leaves, sugar, and lemon juice. Puree until smooth.
  2. Fill 3 glasses wit ice. Add 2 ounces of gin and a few slices of cucumber to each.
  3. Fill the glasses 2/3 full with cucumber mixture.
  4. Top each glass with club soda and stir well.
  5. Garnish with extra mint leaves.

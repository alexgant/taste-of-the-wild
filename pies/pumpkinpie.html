Pumpkin Pie

Delicious any time of year.

"The intense sweetness of a fortified pumpkin makes this dessert popular among children."

Ingredients

  • 1- 9 inch deep dish pie crust. Store bought is fine or make your own.
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • ¼ tsp ground cloves
  • ½ tsp ground salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 15 oz pumpkin puree
  • 12 oz evaporated milk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425℉.
  2. In a small bowl, combine sugar, spices, and salt.
  3. In larger bowl, beat together eggs and evaporated milk.
  4. Add pumpkin puree and mix well.
  5. Add the sugar/spice mixture and mix well.
  6. Pour into uncooked deep dish pie crust, do not overfill.
  7. Bake at 425℉for 15 minutes, then reduce heat to 350℉and bake an additional 40-50 minutes until center is mostly set.
  8. Allow to cool completely before serving.

Fruit Pie

Made with the Berries of Your Choice.

Ingredients

  • 2- 9 inch pie crusts, deep-dish for bottom. Store bought is fine or make your own.
  • 7 cups fresh or frozen mixed berries
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 egg white

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400℉.
  2. In a medium saucepan, cook berries and sugar over medium heat, simmer until warm and juicey (5-10min).
  3. Thicken filling by removing about Â½ cup of juice from pan into bowl and stir in cornstarch until smooth.
  4. Bring pot back to simmer and slowly add cornstarch mixture, stir until smooth.
  5. Remove from heat and let cool.
  6. Pour into unbaked deep dish pie crust. Do not overfill.
  7. Top with second crust, whole with vents cut in, or in a lattice.
  8. In a small bowl, whisk the egg white. Brush over top of crust for a shiny crust.
  9. Bake at 400℉ for 25 minutes. Carefully top with aluminum foil and bake an additional 15 minutes.
  10. Allow to cool completely before serving.

Apple Pie

Sweet Apples and Cinnamon.

"The crispy, flaky pie crust and sweet apples are a match made in heaven."

Ingredients

  • 2- 9 inch pie crusts. Store bought is fine or make your own. One for top and bottom.
  • 6 cups apples, peeled and sliced (granny smith works best, honey crisp is a close second)
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 Tbsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 1 egg white

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350℉
  2. In a large bowl toss prepared apple slices and lemon juice.
  3. In a separate small bowl, mix together sugars and spices.
  4. Melt the butter and add it to the sugar-spice mixture. Add to the bowl of apples and mix well to coat apples evenly.
  5. Pour into pie crust. Top with second crust, whole or in a lattice.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk the egg white. Brush over top of crust for a shiny crust.
  7. Bake at 350℉ for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Cool slightly, serve warm.

Crepes with Honey Syrup

"Sed ut perspiciatis unde omnis iste natus!"

Ingredients

For the crepes:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • canola oil or butter for coating the pan
For the honey syrup:
  • ½ cup butter
  • 4 Tbsp honey
  • 1 Tbsp milk
  • 1 pinch cinnamon
  • 1 pinch nutmeg

Directions

Prepare the crepes:
  1. Add all crepe ingredients to blender. Blend for a 10 second burst until mixed.
  2. If you're not using a blender, sift together sugar, flour, and salt in large bowl.
  3. Whisk in the eggs, vanilla extract, and half of the milk until smooth, then whisk in remaining milk, along with the water and butter.
  4. Batter should be smooth and a uniform consistency.
  5. Once blended, let batter rest in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Brush a non-stick skillet with oil or butter and heat over medium heat.
  7. Pour 1/4 cup of batter into pan, gently tilting and swirling the pan to spread into a large circle of batter.
  8. Cook until the crepe edges begin to look dry and ligthly browned.
  9. Use a thin spatula to flip crepe over and cook on the other side for just a few seconds until golden brown.
  10. Remove crepe and serve with honey syrup.

Prepare the honey syrup:

  1. Melt butter in heat safe bowl in microwave or in small saucepan.
  2. Add honey and mix until smooth.
  3. Add milk, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Mix until smooth.
  4. Serve over fresh, warm crepe.

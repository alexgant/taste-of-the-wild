Nutcake

Spice Cake and candied nut toppings.

Ingredients

For the candied nuts:
  • 1 ½ cup of mixed nuts (walnuts, almonds, pumpkin seeds, etc., or a combination.)
  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • 2 tsp honey
For the spice cake:
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1 ½ cups brown sugar
  • 2 ¼ cups cake flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup applesauce
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla extract

Directions

Prepare the candied nuts:
  1. Preheat the oven to 300℉. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix the nuts, maple syrup, and honey together in a small bowl until the nuts are well coated.
  3. Pour mixture over parchment paper-lined baking sheet. Bake for 5-10 minutes, checking frequently. You want them to be cooked just enough for the maple syrup and honey to caramelize, making the seeds crunchy.
  4. Remove from oven and allow to cool in a dry place for a few hours. The seeds might seem a little soft but will harden once cooled and dried.
Prepare the cake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350℉. Butter and flour 3 8-inch round cake pans.
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar on medium high speed for about 5 minutes until mixture is light in color and fluffy (medium high speed for about 5 minutes). I like to let this cream while doing the next few steps.
  3. In a separate bowl, sift flour, salt, spices, and baking powder together in a large bowl.
  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, to the butter mixture. Mixing after each one until just incorporated.
  5. Add 1/3 of the dry mixture to the butter mixture, beat until well incorporated.
  6. Add 1/2 of the applesauce, beat until well incorporated.
  7. Add vanilla.
  8. Repeat steps 5 and 6 until all ingredients are incorporated (1/3 dry, 1/2 wet, 1/3 dry, 1/2 wet, 1/3 dry).
  9. Scrape sides and bottom of bowl and beat again on medium high for 3 minutes, until mixture is smooth and consistent.
  10. Pour into prepared cake pans.
  11. Bake at 350℉ for 20-25min.
  12. Top with candied nuts.

Carrot Cake

Carrot Cake, cream cheese frosting, candied orange topping.

"This cake is a must for celebrations"

Ingredients

For the carrot cake:
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1 ½ cups brown sugar
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup applesauce
  • 1/2 cup finely grated and drained carrot (1-2 small carrots)
For the cream cheese frosting:
  • 1/2 cup butter, at room temperature
  • 8 oz cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3-4 cups cups powdered sugar
  • 1-3 Tbsp heavy whipping cream (optional)
For the candied orange topping:
  • 4 oranges, sliced into thin circles
  • 12 cups water, divided
  • 4 cups sugar
  • 1 cup ice

Directions

Prepare the candied orange the day before:
  1. Wash and slice the oranges into thin circles.
  2. Add 4 cups of water to a bowl and place at least 1 cup of ice into the bowl and set aside.
  3. In a medium saucepan, bring 4 cups of water to boil.
  4. Place the orange slices into the boiling water for 3 minutes. Quickly remove and put them into the ice bath for 2 minutes. Discard the water.
  5. In the same saucepan, heat 4 cups of sugar and the remaining 4 cups of water to a simmer over medium heat, making sure all the sugar is dissolved.
  6. Place the orange slices back in the simmering sugar water and simmer for 45-60min. Make sure that it is only simmering as we do not want the sugar to crystalize if too much water evaporates. Stir the oranges once every 15 minutes.
  7. Remove oranges, place on a cooling rack and allow to cool and dry for about 24 hours.
  8. Dip the candied oranges in granulated sugar to coat them if desired.
Prepare the cake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350℉. Butter and flour 3 8-inch round cake pans.
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar on medium high speed for about 5 minutes until mixture is light in color and fluffy (medium high speed for about 5 minutes). I like to let this cream while doing the next few steps.
  3. In a separate bowl, sift flour, salt, spices, and baking powder together in a large bowl.
  4. In a separate bowl, grate the carrots using a fine grater; wrap grated carrots in a clean tea towel and squeeze as much water out as you can.
  5. Add one egg to the butter mixture at a time, mix until just incorporated.
  6. Add 1/3 of the dry mixture to the butter mixture, beat until well incorporated.
  7. Add 1/2 of the applesauce, beat until well incorporated.
  8. Add vanilla.
  9. Repeat steps 6 and 7 until all ingredients are incorporated (1/3 dry, 1/2 wet, 1/3 dry, 1/2 wet, 1/3 dry).
  10. Scrape sides and bottom of bowl and beat again on medium high for 3 minutes, until mixture is smooth and consistent.
  11. Stir in grated and drained carrot, mix well.
  12. Pour into prepared cake pans.
  13. Bake at 350℉ for 20-25min.
  14. Let cake cool completely before assembling frosting and topping with candied orange.
Prepare the cream cheese frosting:
  1. Cream the butter and cream cheese together.
  2. Add the vanilla extract.
  3. Sift the powdered sugar one cup at a time into butter and cream cheese mixture, mix on low at first and then medium until well incorporated.
  4. If mixture is too thick, add 1 tbs of heavy whipping cream at a time until desired consistency.
  5. Whip on medium high speed for 2-4 min until light and fluffy.
